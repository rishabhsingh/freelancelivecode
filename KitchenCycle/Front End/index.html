Household Recycling

We re-sell cabinets., refrigerators., ovens., ranges., sinks., microwaves., dishwashers., washers and dryers.

At Kitchen Cycle, our goal is to save cabinetry, appliances and other items from being needlessly scrapped due to the nature of the renovation process. That is why we work with contractors to resell homeowners’ kitchen cabinets and appliances. By using a wide variety of markets to resell these products, we turn clients’ items into cash.
