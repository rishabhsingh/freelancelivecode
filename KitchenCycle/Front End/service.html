Our Appliances

Our collection includes used high-quality appliances and cabinets available for sale. We deal with every brand including Sub-Zero, Wolf, Bosche, Maytag, Legacy USA, Wood-Mode, Wellborn, Dura Supreme, and Merillat. We deal with a wide variety of appliance and cabinet makers!

Hiring KitchenCycle

Upon entering an agreement with Kitchencycle, we will schedule a home visit to photograph and record the dimensions of the items being sold. Any additional information, such as instruction manuals, will help us create more detailed listings for your items. After we complete the listing, we will post it on our website along with several others. We will generate an appropriate asking price based on the itemsâ€™ condition, demand for the products, and original purchase price.

Sold Goods

After your item is sold, we will arrange for the buyer to pick it up. Kitchencyle will notify you of the sale, along with the final price, and schedule a pickup date that works for both parties. If you wish to have your sold inventory removed from your home and picked up elsewhere, we will coordinate an out-of-house pickup with both parties at an additional cost to you. Upon a paid sale, we will send you a check and statement detailing the final sale price of each items, fees incurred from each sale (if any), and your total profit from the entire sale as agreed upon in your contract with Kitchen Cycle.

Other Options

The more time we have to sell your cabinetry and appliances, the more money we tend to receive for the items. Consequently, we advise you to sign up with us as early in the renovation process as possible. If a kitchen is unsold by the time renovation begins, we can have it picked up by our team and brought to our warehouse to be sold on consignment, donated, or disposed.
