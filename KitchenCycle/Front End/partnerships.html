Partnering With KitchenCycle

Thanks for your interest in working with Kitchen Cycle. By connecting us to your clients our services can not only benefit your company and please your customers, but also reduce the burden on our nation's landfills.

Our Process

  • Your referral puts Kitchen Cycle™ in contact with your clients.
  • We meet with your clients and they enter into a resale agreement with our company to sell their items.
  • Our services market their items locally and nationally.
  • Upon a sale, we will inform the project manager or superintendent on the job that we will need to save certain items.
  • To prevent any liability issues, your contractor will be responsible for the removal of any items we have sold to a mutually agreed spot at the job site.
  • Within a week of removal, we will arrange for pick up with the buyers.
  • A Kitchen Cycle™ representative will be on site during the pick up to ensure a seamless delivery.
  • Our company will issue a $250 payment upon sale and delivery of sold items.

Our process is efficient and offers a smooth transition to the start of your clients job. If you are interested in discussing a potential partnership with Kitchen Cycle™, please contact us at your earliest convenience!
